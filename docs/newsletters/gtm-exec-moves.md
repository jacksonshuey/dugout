# GTM / Sales Ops / Executive Moves — Newsletter Coverage

Cross-cutting trade press for the sales profession plus executive-departure signals across every vertical Checkbox AEs sell into. Two jobs to be done:

1. Make the AE sharper at their craft (outbound, discovery, multi-threading, MEDDIC, enterprise tactics).
2. Surface champion-departure signals — the load-bearing demo signal in Dugout. When a CFO, CIO, CISO, GC, or CHRO leaves a target account, deals stall or die. We need newsletters that explicitly publish C-suite hires/departures.

Verified live via WebSearch May 2026. Sender domains marked `(verified)` were confirmed in search results; otherwise marked `(best guess)` and labeled accordingly. Default to plain-HTML body; Substack and beehiiv both qualify.

## Executive-moves deep-dive — the 5 to ingest hardest

For Dugout's champion-departure signal, prioritize these as the highest-density inputs. Webhook them into AgentMail → classify → push to `external_signals` with `signal_type='champion_departure'`.

1. **Boardroom Alpha — Director & Officer Moves Newsletter** — `boardroomalpha.com`. SEC-filing-derived; covers every US public company CEO/CFO/director change. The closest thing to a pure-feed for the signal Dugout cares about.
2. **CFO Dive (Industry Dive)** — `industrydivenews.com` (verified). Daily, free, dense. CFOs are the most frequently named champion in Checkbox demos.
3. **CIO Dive (Industry Dive)** — `industrydivenews.com` (verified). Same Industry Dive infra; CIO/IT-leader appointments.
4. **Fortune CEO Daily + CFO Daily** — `fortune.com`. Two newsletters, one publisher. Pair them. Fortune's "Power Moves" column is the editorial backbone — explicit weekly recaps of gainers/losers in the C-suite.
5. **Axios PM** — `axios.com`. Generalist but explicit about "executive shifts that matter." Catches the household-name moves all of the above miss because they're at private companies or non-public subsidiaries.

**Honorable mention:** *The Daily Upside* — 1M+ subs, finance-flavored, with 22% C-suite readership; routinely covers exec transitions.

## Subscription mechanics & infra notes

- **Industry Dive sender domain** is `industrydivenews.com` — verified. Whitelist that in AgentMail so CFO/CIO/HR Dive don't get spam-filtered.
- **Substack** newsletters (GTMnow, Lenny's, RevOps Co-op, Bowery, possibly others) all send from `*.substack.com`. One whitelist rule covers many.
- **Pavilion's Topline** infrastructure may be on `beehiiv.com` based on `topline.beehiiv.com` redirect seen in search; verify on first email.
- **Paid items flagged:** Lenny's premium ($15/mo), The Information ($399+/yr), Bloomberg Markets Daily / Money Stuff (subscriber-only). Default to free tiers.
- **Forwardability check needed:** Punchbowl Premium and The Information typically include subscriber-specific tracking — confirm forwardability before piping into AgentMail. The Daily Upside, Axios, Fortune, Morning Brew, all Industry Dive properties = plain HTML, fully forwardable.
- **Dead source:** Legal Dive shut down Feb 22, 2025. Do not attempt to subscribe.
