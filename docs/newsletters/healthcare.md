# Healthcare / Payor / Provider Newsletters

**Anchor account:** UnitedHealth Group
**Use case:** signal feed for AEs at Checkbox (legal-tech) selling to in-house legal teams at health insurers, hospital systems, IDNs, and PBMs.
**Pipeline:** AgentMail webhook -> Haiku 4.5 classifier -> `external_signals` -> market-intel browser.

All entries verified as currently active via WebSearch (May 2026). Sender domains are best-guesses based on publisher infrastructure; flagged where confidence is low. Confirm by subscribing one inbox and inspecting headers before wiring the classifier.

- **"Acasa Health"** in the prompt - no newsletter by that name exists. Closest match is **AKASA** (vendor for AI revenue-cycle / prior-auth automation) which publishes a blog, not a true newsletter. Did not include. Worth confirming with the requester whether they meant Akasa or something else.

- **LinkedIn-only newsletters** (e.g., Doximity Insider, several Becker's LI-only feeds) - skipped because LinkedIn often blocks forwarding and breaks the AgentMail webhook pattern.
- **Sheryl Skolnick** - top hospital analyst (now at Optum), but does not publish a newsletter. Worth tracking via X/Twitter or her TWST interviews instead.

## Notes on Signal Density (1-5) scoring

- **5** = breaks original news on payor/PBM/provider deals; AE could cite an article in a discovery call same day
- **4** = strong aggregation + occasional scoops; good context-builder
- **3** = useful background; lower originality
- **1-2** = excluded from this list

Cost flagging: "Free" = no paywall on the newsletter itself, though site articles may be gated. "**Paid**" = newsletter requires subscription.
