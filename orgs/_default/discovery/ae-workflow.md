# AE Workflow — Day in the Life + Information Requirements

## Persona snapshot
Series B/C B2B SaaS AE: 2-5 years quota-carrying experience, $120-160K OTE (50/50 split), $800K-$1.2M annual quota, $40-80K ACV, 30-60 named accounts plus inbound. Per Repvue 2024 benchmarks, only 43% of AEs hit quota — so this rep is anxious, deal-protective, and ruthlessly prioritizes activities they believe move pipe. They optimize daily for *"which 3-5 deals are most likely to close this quarter, and what's the single next action on each?"* Tool fatigue is severe: Gong's 2024 State of Revenue report cites reps using 10+ tools and spending 72% of their week on non-selling activity. They will not log into a new tool unless it tells them something a colleague would tell them.

## A typical day (8am–6pm)
- **8:00-8:30** — Coffee + phone. Slack DMs, email triage, scan Gong "deals at risk" email digest, read replies to last night's Outreach sends. *Mental question: "Did anything blow up overnight?"*
- **8:30-9:30** — Pipeline scrub in SFDC list view (their custom "My Open Pipe" view). Sort by close date. Flag 2-3 deals that need attention today. Update almost nothing.
- **9:30-10:00** — Pre-call prep for first discovery: SFDC opp page → Gong account view → LinkedIn for the prospect → ZoomInfo for org chart → maybe Dock if there's an active room. Pavilion's "Anatomy of a Deal" sessions consistently cite 5-8 minutes of tab-switching per call prep.
- **10:00-12:00** — Back-to-back meetings: discovery, demo with SE, internal deal review. Swyft AI / Gong captures notes.
- **12:00-12:30** — Lunch at desk. Skim LinkedIn for champion job changes, scan news alerts on top-10 accounts.
- **12:30-2:00** — Deal work: follow-up emails, MAP updates in Dock, multi-thread outreach via Outreach. This is where Selected-Vendor-stage deals live or die — chasing legal, security questionnaires, procurement intros.
- **2:00-4:00** — More meetings, plus 30 min of cold/warm outbound to fill next quarter.
- **4:00-5:00** — SFDC hygiene (forced by manager). Stage updates, next-step fields, close date pushes. Done resentfully.
- **5:00-6:00** — Hot deal triage, internal Slack with SE/CS/legal, send the "circling back" email.

## A typical week
- **Mon AM**: weekly pipeline review with manager — every >$50K deal walked through.
- **Tues/Thurs**: forecast submission updates (commit / best case / pipeline).
- **Wed**: 1:1 with manager, 30 min, deal coaching on top 3.
- **Fri PM**: forecast call with VP Sales; rep gets grilled on Selected Vendor and Negotiation stage deals.
- **EOM/EOQ week**: every minute on closing. New-logo prospecting stops.

## Information lookups

### Pre-meeting prep (15 min before discovery/demo)
- **Question:** Who's attending, what've they seen, what changed since last touch?
- **Today:** SFDC → Gong → LinkedIn → ZoomInfo → Dock → email search. 5-8 min.
- **Friction:** Misses things; no synthesis; doesn't know if Dock pricing PDF was opened.
- **10x:** One pre-call brief auto-generated 15 min before the calendar event.

### Post-meeting debrief
- **Question:** What did I commit to, what's the next step, who needs to be looped in?
- **Today:** Swyft/Gong auto-capture → manually re-type into SFDC next-step field.
- **Friction:** Double entry; commitments slip if not Slacked immediately.
- **10x:** Auto-draft of next-step + follow-up email + SFDC update from call.

### Monday pipeline review prep
- **Question:** For every deal >$50K — what changed last week, what's the risk, what's my ask?
- **Today:** Eyeballs SFDC, scrolls Gong account timelines, guesses.
- **Friction:** Walks into review without crisp "what changed."
- **10x:** Weekly per-deal change log: stage moves, new contacts, engagement deltas, competitor mentions.

### Quarter-end deal triage
- **Question:** Which Selected Vendor / Negotiation deals are actually closing?
- **Today:** Manager's gut + AE's gut + Gong "deal warnings."
- **Friction:** Gong warnings are too noisy; everyone disables them by week 3.
- **10x:** Procurement/legal/security-track engagement signals specifically — the wedge.

### Cold account warming
- **Question:** Which of my 60 accounts had a buying signal this week?
- **Today:** ZoomInfo Intent tab (rarely opened), HubSpot anonymous-visit reports (never opened).
- **Friction:** Two separate logins; no account-match confidence.
- **10x:** Daily 5-account "warm now" list filtered to named book.

### Inbound response triage
- **Question:** Is this lead real, is it in my territory, who else from the account is engaged?
- **Today:** HubSpot lead record → SFDC account match → Slack RevOps.
- **Friction:** Sub-5-minute response wins inbound (Chili Piper / Drift data); manual lookup blows the window.
- **10x:** Inbound enriched + routed + context-bundled in <60 sec.

### Champion-changed alert
- **Question:** Did my champion leave / get promoted / change scope?
- **Today:** Nothing systematic. Finds out from a bounced email or a LinkedIn notification weeks late.
- **Friction:** Single biggest cause of deal death post-Selected Vendor.
- **10x:** Real-time alert with new role + replacement suggestion from buying committee.

### Competitive threat response
- **Question:** Did Ironclad/LinkSquares come up on the last 3 calls?
- **Today:** Would require scrubbing Gong transcripts manually. Doesn't happen.
- **Friction:** Rep finds out about competitive eval in the loss debrief.
- **10x:** Competitor-mention timeline per opp with quote + speaker + call link.

### Multi-thread health check
- **Question:** Am I single-threaded? Who haven't I met?
- **Today:** Mental model + LinkedIn org chart guess.
- **Friction:** Gong has buying-committee data; AEs don't query it.
- **10x:** Buying committee coverage map auto-generated per opp.

### Procurement / legal stage chase
- **Question:** Where's the MSA, who's blocking, did Finance open the order form?
- **Today:** Email threads + DocuSign status + Dock if remembered.
- **Friction:** *This is the Selected Vendor wedge.* Nothing aggregates legal/security/finance engagement.
- **10x:** Procurement-track dashboard: doc opens, redline turnaround time, security-q status, exec sponsor activity.

### Inbound news / trigger event
- **Question:** Did my account just announce funding / layoff / exec hire?
- **Today:** Manually-scanned newsletters, ZoomInfo Scoops.
- **Friction:** Buried in inbox; never tied to specific opp.
- **10x:** Account news pinned to opp record with suggested outreach angle.

## Daily decisions

| Decision | Needs to know | Freshness | Cost of wrong |
|---|---|---|---|
| Which 5 deals to work today? | Engagement deltas across pipe | Same day | Missed close |
| Push for meeting this week or next? | Champion + committee readiness | 24h | Stall |
| Skip or attend internal review? | Recent activity per opp | Same day | Manager loses confidence |
| Send pricing now or after demo? | Buyer's stage signals + comp threat | 24h | Discount given unnecessarily |
| Escalate to VP Sales sponsor? | Stalled days + decision-maker silence | 48h | Deal dies quietly |
| Loop in CS/SE? | Technical questions in transcripts | Same week | Slow response loses momentum |
| Forecast commit vs best case? | Procurement/legal stage progress | Weekly | Forecast miss = career risk |
| Disqualify and reallocate time? | No-activity threshold per stage | Weekly | Wasted Q |

## What AEs WANT but can't easily find
- Did Finance/Procurement actually open the order form / pricing PDF? (Dock has it; never checked.)
- Is my champion still employed there? (ZoomInfo Scoops; not in daily flow.)
- Which competitor surfaced in the last 3 calls and what was said? (Gong; requires transcript scrub.)
- Are other buying-committee members talking to us, or just my champion? (Gong + HubSpot; not unified.)
- Has this account churned from a competitor in the last 90 days? (LinkedIn / news; manual.)
- What did the security questionnaire say last time we sold to this vertical? (Tribal knowledge.)

## Anti-requirements (do NOT build)
- Generic intent without confident account match — noise.
- Gong coaching metrics surfaced to the rep (those are manager-facing).
- Marketing attribution debates (MQL source).
- Long-form weekly email digests — won't open past week 2.
- Real-time Slack pings for every signal — alert fatigue kills adoption.
- "AI-suggested next email" without context grounding — reps spot generic copy instantly.

## Top 3 questions for /ask
1. "Why is the Helios deal stalling?"
2. "Which of my deals lost momentum this week and why?"
3. "Who at Acme have we not yet engaged that we should?"

## Top 3 information surfaces
1. **Pre-Call Brief** — auto-rendered 15 min before any external meeting: who's attending, what they've seen/opened, what changed since last touch, suggested opener. Changes the 8-min tab-juggle into a 60-sec read.
2. **Selected-Vendor Procurement Tracker** — per-opp view of legal/security/finance engagement (doc opens, redline cycle time, days-since-last-procurement-touch, exec sponsor activity). Directly addresses the wedge; surfaces dying deals 2 weeks earlier.
3. **Daily Deal Delta** — one Slack message at 8am: "3 things changed on your pipe overnight" (champion job change, competitor mentioned, finance opened order form). Replaces Gong's noisy warnings with high-signal, named-book-only alerts.
